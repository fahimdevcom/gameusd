Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I get gameUSD?

gameUSD can be acquired by a few methods. If you are a web3 game developer, web3 gaming studio or investor of a certain stature and meet the requisite criteria, you can directly purchase gameUSD from gameUSD foundation. gameUSD foundation does not sell tokens directly to retail customers. It is essential for any users to conduct research and due diligence before making such a decision.

How can I trust that gameUSD will always be equivalent to a USDT?

Each gameUSD is fully backed by one USDT. These assets are safely held by gameUSD foundation’s treasury, audited and multi-signatory to ensure security and transparency. This approach is what provides gameUSD with its stability and its equivalent value to the USDT.

How can I redeem gameUSD?

To redeem your gameUSD stablecoin to USDT, 1% redemption fees will be imposed upon redemption and you must first meet requirements, including Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Counter-Terrorism Financing (CTF) processes. Upon successful completion of these due diligence checks, you can exchange your gameUSD for its equivalent in USDT, thereby taking gameUSD out of circulation. Please be aware of all legal and financial regulations in your jurisdiction before proceeding.
